The Archetypes

Think of these as Social Shields. If you feel shy or exposed, activate an Archetype to hide behind a character while sharing your truth.

  • The Detective: Ask "Why?" a lot. Be curious.
  • The Comedian: Make it funny, even if it's sad.
  • The Stoic: Share facts, but show no emotion.
  • The Dramatic: Make everything sound intense.
